20. Recruitment Resources Hub

20.0 Purpose

This hub serves as a centralized, one-stop-shop for all templates, tools, and guides related to the recruitment and selection process. It is designed to help recruiters and hiring managers run a fair, efficient, and effective hiring process from start to finish.

20.1 Resource Library Structure

  • Templates:
  • Job Description Template (with inclusive language prompts)
  • Phone Screen Questionnaire Template
  • Behavioral Interview Kit Template (by competency)
  • Offer Letter Template (KSA Compliant)
  • Rejection Email Template (with positive candidate experience focus)
  • Checklists:
  • Hiring Manager Kick-off Meeting Checklist
  • Interview Panel Briefing Checklist
  • Candidate Experience Audit Checklist
  • Calculators & Tools:
  • Cost-per-Hire Calculator
  • Agency Fee vs. In-house Cost Modeler
  • Recruitment Funnel Conversion Rate Analyzer
  • Guides:
  • Guide to Writing Inclusive Job Descriptions
  • Mastering the STAR Method
  • A Hiring Manager's Guide to Reducing Bias
