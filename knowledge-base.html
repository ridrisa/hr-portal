Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the knowledge base updated?

Our knowledge base is updated monthly with new articles, and existing content is reviewed quarterly to ensure accuracy and relevance.

Can I contribute to the knowledge base?

Yes! We welcome contributions from HR professionals. Please contact us through the community section to submit your article for review.

Are the templates in the resource section editable?

Yes, all templates are provided in editable formats. You can download and modify them to suit your organization's needs.

Is content available in both English and Arabic?

Currently, most articles are available in English, with key resources available in Arabic. We are working to provide bilingual versions of all content.
